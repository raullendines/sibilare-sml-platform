# Pricing Model

Pricing is client-specific and should be based on:

- Platforms enabled.
- Own brands and subbrands tracked.
- Competitors tracked.
- Estimated monthly mention volume.
- Data refresh frequency.
- Maximum posts per period.
- Chatbot message limit.
- Reports and presentations.
- AI pattern detection.
- Managed service complexity.

## Volume Estimation

Use Meltwater or another source to estimate expected monthly mentions per brand and platform.

The estimate should feed:

- Suggested pricing tier.
- Suggested multiplier.
- Maximum posts per period.
- Monthly chatbot limit.
- Cost budget defaults.

## Cost Control

Every cost-bearing operation should write to `usage_ledger`, including:

- Apify runs.
- AI classification.
- Chatbot messages.
- Report generation.
- Exports.
- Pattern detection.
