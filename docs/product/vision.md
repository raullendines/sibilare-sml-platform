# Product Vision

The goal is to provide a proprietary platform for representing Social Media Listening data for different clients.

The product is not a generic SaaS. It is a managed and personalized service. Each client can have:

- Custom dashboards.
- Custom chart configurations.
- Custom topics and subproducts.
- Custom AI-generated texts.
- Custom branding with colors and logos.
- Custom competitors and benchmarking views.
- Custom extraction frequency, platforms, and maximum posts.

## Core Challenges

1. Personalization must be real, not just a white-label theme.
2. Apify dependency must be controlled through fallback agents, cost tracking, limits, jobs, retries, and monitoring.
3. Pricing must account for estimated conversation volume. Tracking Coca-Cola is not equivalent to tracking a small local brand.
4. Reports and presentations must be reproducible through stored snapshots.
5. AI output must be auditable and reviewable.

## Supported Platforms

- X
- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube
- News

## Known Limitations

- No historical backfill is promised by default.
- The service promises a maximum number of extracted posts, generally the most relevant ones, not full conversation coverage.
